## Automotive Inventory Ads (AIA)

## What is AIA?

AIA or Automotive Inventory Ads are a NEW and exciting feature Facebook has introduced to help dealerships achieve even higher results on the Marketplace AND MORE!

## What do they do and how can they help my dealership?

- Increase traffic to your dealership's Facebook page
- You can see at least 50% increase in message volume (sometimes even more!)
- Less tire kickers and MORE serious buyers!
- Get your dealership's name noticed more in your area!

## How will I receive leads from these advertisements?

When a customer finds your ad on any of the platforms that your vehicles are displayed on, it will take the customer directly to the Marketplace posting for your vehicle. From there, the customer will be able to send you a direct message to your Facebook. Hammer will be able to answer these messages like any other Facebook message and will be able to create a lead and send to your CRM!

## What do they look like?

On Facebook Feed, Instagram/Facebook Stories, Instagram Feed, and Marketplace.

## How does it work?

Have you ever been shopping online for an item and then logged onto Facebook to see that very item being marketed to you on a sponsored ad? Welcome to the Dealership version of this! Your ads will be targeting a demographic that Facebook has already done all the research, analyzed and used to bring you customers that are actively looking to purchase a new vehicle. By doing this, you'll have MORE serious buyers and LESS tire kickers.

## How much does it cost?

There are two possible costs you will incur:

1. **Hammer Fee** — Discussed with a Hammer representative; can vary depending on the dealership. This fee allows access to your Inventory Catalog. Without this access you'll be unable to use AIA services with Facebook.
2. **Cost to Facebook** — Separate cost varying by reach. Hammer recommends **$20 per day, 4 days a week ($80 per week)** to see impact.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I customize who my ads reach?

To an extent, yes. Your ads will automatically reach customers who are looking for vehicles; you can change targeting options shown in Facebook's ad tools. A gauge shows potential reach in your area.

### How many miles away can my ads be seen?

Default radius up to **50 miles**. You can run multiple AIA ads aimed in different directions (e.g. north, east, west, south) to cover more ground.

### Can I pick what cars are displayed?

No — it is a **carousel of your inventory, up to 25 vehicles** at a time. If you have more than 25 vehicles, the system automatically rotates inventory.

### How can I see my results?

Results are in your **Ad Center on Facebook**.

### Can I tell which messages are from AIA and other messages?

Short answer, no. At this time Hammer is still working with Facebook to tweak the product; changes may allow this later but there is no confirmed timeline.
